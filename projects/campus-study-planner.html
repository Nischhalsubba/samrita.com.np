Project Practice

Campus Study Planner

A study-planning exercise that shows organization, consistency, and self-management during civil engineering learning.

FocusPlanning
OutputWeekly study rhythm
RoleSelf-management

Purpose

This project translates academic pressure into a visible weekly plan. It is included because recruiters often value freshers who can manage deadlines and maintain consistency.

What was practiced

  • Breaking large subjects into weekly tasks
  • Balancing drawing practice, theory, and revision
  • Tracking pending work honestly
  • Reviewing progress instead of relying on memory

Engineering value

Planning habits transfer directly to site work: daily progress tracking, task prioritization, and clear follow-up on pending decisions.

Next improvement

The planner can be improved with downloadable templates for study logs, site task logs, and weekly review notes.
